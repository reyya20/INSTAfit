Instructions for Hand Curls (Bicep Curls)

To perform hand curls (bicep curls) correctly:

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, holding dumbbells in both hands.
  2. Keep your elbows close to your body and palms facing forward.
  3. Slowly lift the dumbbells towards your shoulders, bending at the elbows.
  4. Pause at the top and then slowly lower the dumbbells back to the starting position.
  5. Repeat steps 3-4.

Ensure to maintain proper posture and avoid swinging your body to lift the weights.
